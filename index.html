What is SCIL?

SCIL is the Smart City Infrastructure Lab at NTNU.

What is Makerspace-IDI?

NTNU Department of Computer Science (aka. IDI) has its own Makerspace located in IT-Bygget room 142, ground floor.

It is equipped with tools and components so that you easily may get to work on your ideas.

Dirk Ahlers is overseeing the continuous development of the Makerspace.

The makerspace is run by a group of students, keeping the space and tools operationable and up to date. They are also very friendly and may answer any questions you may have.
